- Molecule is the smallest particle that exits independently.
	- When an atom of an element combines with atom of another or same element.
	- Types
		- Monoatomic: $He$, $Ne$
		- Diatomic: $H_2$, $O_2$, $Cl_2$
		- Triatomic: $O_3$, $CO_2$
		- Polyatomic: $P_4$, $S_8$
