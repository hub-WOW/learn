- Dobereiner was a German Scientist.
- Elements arranged in groups of three called triads.
- Atomic mass of middle element ≈ average of the other two.
- Elements in a triad show similar chemical properties.
  
  | Element 1 | Element 2 (Middle) | Element 3 | Mass Check |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | Li (7) | Na (23) | K (39) | (7 + 39)/2 = 23 |
  | Ca (40) | Sr (88) | Ba (137) | (40 + 137)/2 ≈ 88 |
  | Cl (35.5) | Br (80) | I (127) | (35.5 + 127)/2 ≈ 81 |
- Drawbacks
	- Many exceptions like {$Fe$, $Mn$, $Ni$}, and {$Co$, $Zn$, $Cu$}
