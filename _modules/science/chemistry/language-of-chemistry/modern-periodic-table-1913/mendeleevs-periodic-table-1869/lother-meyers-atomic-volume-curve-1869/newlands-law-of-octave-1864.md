- Made by John Newland.
- Elements arranged in increasing atomic mass.
- Every 8th element has properties similar to the first.
- Named after musical octaves.
  
  | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 |
  |---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
  | Li | Be | B | C | N | O | F | Na |
  | Mg | Al | Si | P | S | Cl | K | Ca |
- Drawbacks
	- Accurate till $Ca$ only.
	- Many inaccuracies.
