- Ions are charged atoms or compounds.
- When an atom or molecule gains or loses electrons, it forms an ion.
- Usually formed in atoms due to [Octate Rule](/courses/science/chemistry/electronic-configuration-of-atom/bohr-burry-scheme/octate-rule.html).
- Polyatomic Ions
	- Known as compound radicals.
	- They have more than 1 Atom.
	- Example: $NH_4$
- Cations
	- Positively Charged.
	- Electrons are lost.
