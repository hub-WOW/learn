- Carbon can form allotropes, which means that it can form structures without any other element.
- Allotropes
	- | Allotrope | Type |
	  |---|---|
	  | Diamond | Morphous |
	  | Graphite | Morphous |
	  | Fullerene | Morphous |
	  |**---**|**---**|**---**|
	  | Coal | Amorphous|
	  | Charcoal | Amorphous |
	  | Coke | Amorphous |
	- Morphous
		- Crystalline Structure
		- Long Chains
		- Pure
		- Definite Geometric Shape
		- 1 Melting Point
		- Hard to Destroy
	- Amorphous
		- Have impurities like Sulphur and Nitrogen
		- Short Chains
		- Regular Arrangement
		- Range of Melting Points
